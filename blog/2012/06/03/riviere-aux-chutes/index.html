Riviere Aux Chutes

Jun 3rd, 2012

Amazingly awesome weekend playing, in and around the Deschutes River.  Sometimes I can’t believe how lucky I am to walk out my front door, walk 5 minutes, and be fishing in such a stunning place.

My stoke only got higher when I landed my first fish (minnow?  it was like 5” long), and was equally as stoked when the second one (8 inches of pure furry) gulped up my parachute adams.  So fantastic to be waded out in the river, casting away.

Finished up Saturday with a family bike ride to the park, a mere stroll from my little fishing hole.

Today I started off the day running down the river trail and then I got to fish the deschutes near FS road 40.  The skeeters were fierce, and even without deet I managed to land a 9” brown.

So.

Much.

Fun.
