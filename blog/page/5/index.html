Total Eclipse of the Gnar

Dec 10th, 2011

Had another wonderful dat Saturday watching Kaydee learn to ski.  Camping out the night before in the RV was super fun.  Getting to watch the eclipse after Kaydee woke me up at 4am asking if it was time to wake up was a nice bonus too.

Waking up in the ‘spirit’ and walking up to the hill was a fantastic experience.  Hot cocco in the lodge before hand and watching Kaydee’s stoke level go through to roof was quite memorable too. In her lesson she was snow-plowin like a champ!  I’m amazed at how fast kids pick up things like skiing, and I’m thankful we live here in Bend where I can give the girls the opprotunity to grow up skiing.

The turns for me weren’t too stellar… the coverage is getting super thin, but still fun to ski.  Becky and Teagan came up to watch Kaydee ride the magic carpet and ignore the teacher’s request to stop too.

Finished out the perfect day by going out on date night with Becky.  The only thing that could have made the day better was 6” of freshies, but it was pretty damned good as is.

First Tracks

Dec 3rd, 2011

I will never forget today.  You see today was my first dose of “Holy Crap, my little baby girl is growing up” served with a prideful side of “Wow Kaydee is skiing!”  Indeed as I walked away after dropping off my little bear for her first ski lesson ever, the emotions were flooding over me.  It was a magical moment, in a place that means alot to me.

During her lesson I was able to go out and ski with some buddies again, and have a great day… but the best moments were sharing a PB&J with her at lunch.  Seeing her excitement to get back out on the snow.  And watching her slide down the snow between her teacher’s legs.

And my little girl, who I thought would be asleep from all the days exertion and excitement, is still running around the house… obviously high from the stoke of the day, and experiencing the freedom of glisse for the first time.

So proud, so happy.
